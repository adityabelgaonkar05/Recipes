Lasagna

Lasagna is one of the most delicious dishes one can make at home.
Without further adieu, let's get to the recipe!

For this recipe you'll need:

  • 1 pound (450g) of ground beef or a mix of beef and pork
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 24-32 ounces (680-900g) of marinara sauce or homemade tomato sauce
  • Lasagna noodles (around 9-12 pieces)
  • 15 ounces (425g) of ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (200g) of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (50g) of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Italian seasoning (optional)
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground meat over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Cook until the meat is browned and the onions are translucent.
    Drain excess fat if needed.
  3. Stir in the marinara sauce and let the mixture simmer for about 10-15 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to taste.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella, Parmesan cheese, and an egg. Add a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  5. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Make sure they are al dente as they'll continue to cook in the oven. Drain and set aside.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  7. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Add another layer of the meat sauce.
  10. Repeat the layers: noodles, cheese mixture, meat sauce until all ingredients are used, ending with a final layer of meat sauce on top.
  11. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the top.
  12. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for about 25-30 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
  14. Let the lasagna sit for 10-15 minutes before slicing to allow it to set.
  15. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired and serve.

There you have it, Fresh home-made lasagna is ready!
