Poutine

We will learn how to make some very delicious Poutine!

For this recipe you will need:

  • 1 bag (about 24 oz) of frozen French fries or 4 large potatoes for homemade fries
  • 2 cups of cheese curds (substitute with fresh mozzarella or another melty cheese if needed)
  • 2 cups of beef or chicken gravy (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the frozen French fries following the instructions on the package or prepar e homemade fries by peeling and cutting 4 large potatoes into thin strips. Fry until golden brown and crispy.
  2. While the fries are cooking, heat 2 cups of beef or chicken gravy on the stovetop until it's hot and simmering.
  3. Once the fries are done, arrange them on a serving plate or individual plates.
  4. Sprinkle 2 cups of cheese curds evenly over the hot fries.
  5. Pour the hot gravy over the fries and cheese curds, ensuring the cheese starts to melt from the heat.
  6. Serve immediately, allowing the flavors to meld, and enjoy your delectable poutine!

Feel free to add any desired toppings for a personal touch. and there you have it! Poutine right at your home!
